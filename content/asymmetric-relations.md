### Asymmetric Relations
A [relation](relations.md) $R$ is _asymmetric_ if each pair of different elements does **not** have its inverse in the relation.
$$
xRy \land yRx
$$

#### Example
$$
\begin{align}
	A&=\{1,2,3\} \\
	R&=\{(1,1), (2,2), (1,2), (3,1),(3,3)\}
\end{align}
$$
