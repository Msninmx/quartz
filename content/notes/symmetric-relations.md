### Symmetric Relations
A [[notes/relations|relation]] $R$ is _symmetric_ if each pair of different elements has its inverse in the relation.
$$
xRy \to yRx
$$

#### Example
$$
\begin{align}
	A&=\{1,2,3\} \\
	R&=\{(1,3), (2,1), (1,2), (3,1), (1,1)\}
\end{align}
$$
