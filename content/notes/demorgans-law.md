### De Morgan's Law
$$
\begin{rcases}
	(P \cup Q)^c = P^c \cap Q^c \\
	(P \cap Q)^c = P^c \cup Q^c
\end{rcases} =
\text{DeMorgan's Law}
$$
[[notes/set-union|Union]] $\cup$ is can be considered as addition $+$, and [[notes/set-intersection|intersection]] $\cap$ is multiplication $\times$.
