### Cartesian Product
Let $A$ and $B$ be two non empty [sets](set-union.md). The Cartesian Product of these sets is the set of ordered pairs, which in turn, becomes a [relation](relations.md).
$$
A \times B=\{ (a,b)|a \in A \land b \in B \}
$$
