### Transitive Relations
A [[notes/relations|relation]] $R$ is _transitive_ if we can find a [[notes/laws-of-logical-inference|hypothetical syllogism]] within its elements.
$$
(xRy)\land(yRz) \to xRz
$$

#### Example
$$
\begin{align}
	A&=\{1,2,3\} \\
	R&=\{(1,2), (2,3),(1,3)\}
\end{align}
$$
