### Matrix
Matrices are rectangular arrangements of numbers.
$$
A =
\begin{bmatrix}
	a_{11} & a_{21} & \cdots & a_{1n} \\
	a_{21} & a_{31} & \cdots & a_{2n} \\
	\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
	a_{m1} & a_{m2} & \cdots & a_{mn} \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
The matrix $A$ is $m \times n$ big.
